# School TV Daily Operations Guide

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgrperna7/image/upload/v1774312056/samples/animals/reindeer.jpg)

## Morning Setup: Turning on the TVs

When you arrive at school, you need to turn on and set up the three digital signage TVs.

**TV Locations:**

* Reception area
* Classroom block: Between Class 3 and Class 4
* Classroom block: Between Class 5 and Class 6

**Step-by-Step Setup:**

1. **Locate the remote:** The remote control can be found in one of the classrooms.
2. **Navigate the menu:** Turn on the TV and use the remote to go to **Input** > **Media Player** > **Use**.
3. **Play the video:** Select and run one of the "street vid" files.
4. **Set the loop:** > **Important:** You must ensure the video settings are set to **Repeat Single** or **Repeat One** so the video loops continuously throughout the day.

***

## End of Day: Shutdown

At the end of the school day, simply grab the remote and ensure all three TVs (Reception, Block 3-4, and Block 5-6) are turned off before you leave.
