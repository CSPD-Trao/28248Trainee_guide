## Start of Day

***

## Classroom Walk-Around

Try to run around at least 20 times a day just to show presence

***

### Rules for Live Documents

* Always save your changes immediately after making them
* Never eat a document open and unsaved for long periods
* If you are unsure whether something needs updating, update it anyway and flag it to your NTSO
* Do not share live documents outside of CSPD

***

## Tickets

Every job must have a ticket. This applies to planned work, reactive work, and anything flagged during a walk-around. No job should ever be completed without a ticket being raised first. Try to encourage Teachers to make tickets!

### Rules for Tickets

* Raise a ticket before starting any job
* Keep the ticket updated as the job progresses
* Mark the ticket as complete as soon as the job is finished
* If a job is blocked or delayed, update the ticket with the reason
* Review all open tickets at the start and end of every day
