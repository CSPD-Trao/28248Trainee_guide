# General Jobs

A daily checklist of tasks to be completed.

---

## Start of Day

These tasks must be completed before trainees arrive and the day begins.

- [ ] Turn ON ALL 3 TVs throughout the building
- [ ] Check that all TVs are displaying the correct content
- [ ] Review any outstanding tickets from the previous day

---

## Classroom Walk-Around

A walk-around must be completed at least once in the morning and once in the afternoon. The purpose is to ensure all classrooms are happy with their Tech.

---

### Rules for Live Documents

- Always save your changes immediately after making them
- Never leave a document open and unsaved for long periods
- If you are unsure whether something needs updating, update it anyway and flag it to your supervisor
- Do not share live documents outside of CSPD

---

## Tickets

Every job must have a ticket. This applies to planned work, reactive work, and anything flagged during a walk-around. No job should ever be completed without a ticket being raised first.

### Rules for Tickets

- [ ] Raise a ticket before starting any job
- [ ] Keep the ticket updated as the job progresses
- [ ] Mark the ticket as complete as soon as the job is finished
- [ ] If a job is blocked or delayed, update the ticket with the reason
- [ ] Review all open tickets at the start and end of every day

### What a Good Ticket Includes

- A clear title describing the job
- The date it was raised
- The name of the person responsible
- The current status (Open, In Progress, Blocked, Completed)
- Any notes or updates as the job progresses

---

## End of Day

These tasks must be completed before leaving at the end of the day.

- [ ] Turn OFF ALL TVs throughout the building
- [ ] Ensure all tickets are updated with the latest status
- [ ] Lock all doors and turn off AC before leaving
